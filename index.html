TIC TAC TOE GAME

Tips:

  • Tap any space when 'no more' space left.
  • Spaces will be cleared after getting a winner.
  • After the first win, "loser" starts next;
    - if player 'X' started and won the next player is '0' and vice versa.

Rules:

  • Winning when your friend has no more space board is NOT a win.
  • NO repeating of the occupied board.
